About Us

Nurturing Excellence in Shahpur Chakar Since Our Foundation

The Smart School is a proud project of The City School network — one of Pakistan's most reputed educational institutions. Located in the heart of Shahpur Chakar, Sindh, we are dedicated to providing world-class education with a local understanding and global perspective.

Our motto, "Tomorrow is our Destiny," reflects our unwavering commitment to preparing students not just for examinations, but for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow's world.

  • Academic Excellence

    City School curriculum ensuring national and international standards.

  • Character Building

    Islamic values, ethics and social responsibility at the core.

  • Modern Learning

    Technology-enhanced classrooms and smart learning tools.

  • Community & Family

    Strong parent-school partnership for holistic student growth.

School Uniform

Dress Code & Uniform Kit

Uniform promotes discipline, equality and school pride. All students must adhere to the prescribed dress code.

Boys — Summer Uniform
  • White half-sleeve shirt with school logo
  • Navy blue trousers (formal cut)
  • Black leather shoes (polished)
  • White socks (above ankle)
  • Navy blue school tie
  • School monogram belt
Girls — Summer Uniform
  • White shalwar kameez with school logo
  • White dupatta (compulsory)
  • White flat shoes or sandals
  • White socks
  • Hair neatly tied or covered
  • No jewellery allowed
Winter Uniform (Boys & Girls)
  • Navy blue school sweater/pullover
  • School blazer (Class V & above)
  • White full-sleeve shirt underneath
  • Navy blue trousers / shalwar
  • Black shoes (polished)
  • School scarf optional in severe cold
Physical Education (P.E.) Kit
  • White school T-shirt with logo
  • Navy blue track trousers
  • White sports shoes (clean)
  • White sports socks
  • School sports bag
  • PE kit mandatory on PE days only
School Bag & Accessories
  • Navy blue or black school bag
  • School diary (compulsory daily)
  • Water bottle (own, labelled)
  • Lunch box (no outside food)
  • Stationery in school pencil case
  • No fancy bags or trolley bags
Uniform Rules & Compliance
  • Uniform compulsory every school day
  • Dirty/torn uniform will be noted
  • Dress code checked at school gate
  • 3 violations = parent notification
  • No coloured hair, nail polish, or tattoos
  • Special dress on designated event days
